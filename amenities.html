Some hotels offer "free" breakfast, but obviously this is built into their room rate. Hence, the breakfast is not really "free"; rather, it is "compulsory". Our room prices do NOT include breakfast. Every day, you can choose whether to have a big breakfast, a small one, or none at all, and pay only for what you consume. Our daily buffet theme breakfast is priced at P 950 Nett * (smaller children half-price), while a plated American, Continental or Filipino breakfast costs P 495 Nett *. Fruits or Pancakes cost P 275 Nett *, and a cup of brewed coffee to take away costs P132 Nett *.
